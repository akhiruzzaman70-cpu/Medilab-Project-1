Frequently Asked Questions

Medilab FAQs — Because Informed Patients Make Healthier Choices.

How do I book an online doctor appointment?

Booking a consultation on Medilab is simple. Just visit our website or mobile app, select a medical department or doctor, choose your preferred date and time, and confirm your booking. You’ll receive an email or SMS confirmation with the appointment details and a secure consultation link.

Are the doctors on Medilab qualified?

Yes. All doctors on Medilab are verified and certified medical professionals with years of clinical experience. We carefully vet every doctor before listing them on our platform to ensure high-quality and trustworthy medical care.

Can I get a prescription from an online consultation?

Absolutely. After the consultation, the doctor may issue an electronic prescription based on your symptoms and condition. You can download this prescription or use it directly at any pharmacy to purchase your medicine.

Is online consultation as effective as visiting a doctor in person?

Yes, for most common health conditions, online consultations are just as effective as in-person visits. Our doctors can assess your symptoms, provide medical advice, prescribe medications, and recommend further action if necessary. For emergencies, we recommend immediate offline or hospital care.

How much does an online consultation cost?

Consultation fees vary based on the doctor’s experience and specialty. The exact fee is shown before you confirm your booking. There are no hidden charges. Medilab often offers affordable plans and health packages for regular users.

Is my health information safe and private?

Yes, your data is 100% secure. Medilab uses end-to-end encryption and follows strict privacy protocols. We never share your personal or medical information with any third party without your consent.

What types of doctors are available on Medilab?

Medilab offers a wide range of specialists, including General Physicians, Pediatricians, Gynecologists, Psychiatrists, Dermatologists, Cardiologists, ENT Specialists, Neurologists, Orthopedic Doctors, Gastroenterologists, Urologists, and more.

Is Medilab available 24/7?

Yes. Our platform operates 24/7. While doctor availability depends on their schedule, you can book consultations at any time, including weekends and holidays.

Do I need to download anything for the consultation?

No special software is required. Consultations can be done via your browser or phone. A good internet connection, camera, and microphone will ensure a smooth video call experience.
